Welcome to our Farm

Welcome to Blackshaw Gap: a charming new cottage conversion, situated on our 33 acre farm in the Cheshire Peak District. Dating from the 17th century, the farm has been thoughtfully renovated over the last decade.

Named after one of our fields, Blackshaw Gap comprises a self-contained living/kitchen space and an en-suite bedroom above. Warmed by underfloor heating, the living room includes a comfortable Tetrad sofa, an antique French dining table, and a curated selection of local books and maps.

Rest & Retreat

Upstairs, the en-suite bedroom features a beautiful vaulted ceiling and sweeping views of the valley. Enjoy a restful night's sleep in our super king (6') bed.

The Shaker-style kitchen area has a wonderful country feel and is fully equipped with a Nespresso Vertuo coffee maker, induction hob, Breville air fryer, and beautiful Emma Bridgewater crockery sourced from the nearby Potteries.

Farmhouse Dining

As passionate foodies, we are delighted to offer optional dining experiences to make your stay extra special:

  • Breakfast: A light continental breakfast delivered to your fridge, or hot options served in the cottage.
  • Picnic Lunches: Perfect if you are heading out to explore the Gritstone Trail or Lyme Park.
  • Grazing Boards: Cosy up with a bottle of wine and enjoy a hearty sharing platter.
  • One Pot Dishes: Delivered to your fridge during the day for you to reheat at your convenience.

Cottage Highlights

The Great Outdoors

33 acres of non-working farm to explore. Meet our Hebridean sheep, miniature Shetland ponies, and hens. Weber BBQ available on request.

Modern Comforts

Underfloor heating, dedicated workspace, fast WiFi, and a well-equipped shaker-style kitchen.

Prime Location

Just 5 minutes from Bollington, nestled inside the Peak District National Park. Walk to White Nancy or hike the Goyt Valley.
